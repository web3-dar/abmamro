We are going GREEN!

In our effort to go paperless and to preserve the environment, we will be converting your hardcopy account statements to e-statements starting 2nd March, 2025*. With e-statements, you can view and save your statements online anytime and anywhere, at your convenience.

For Amro Bank Online users, if you prefer to continue receiving hardcopy statements via post, please log onto Amro Bank Online to change the settings after 3th March, 2025 onwards or contact Amro Bank for assistance.

*Applicable to Amro Bank Online users only. Currently, this service is only applicable for Current/Savings Account, Multi Currency Account & Credit Card.
